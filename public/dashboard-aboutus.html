What is DLSU SCoOp?

DLSU Student Co-Operative (SCoOp) is the first 100% student-owned, student-operated, and student-built business wihin a university in the Philippines.

As a student co-operative, SCoOp trains its members to provide services for the student body and become future Lasallian entrepreneurs.

Since the pandemic, SCoOp has resumed various services such as paper and tarpaulin printing, lamination, and ring binding to DLSU students and offices.

SCoOp also conducts in-person and virtual internal events, including general assemblies, game nights, and personal development seminars for its members.
